About William Lu

I am currently a Sophomore student at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, studying Computer Science and Mathematics. As I navigate through my academic journey, I find myself being attracted to the field of Artificial Intelligence and Video Game Development.　The convergence of these two cutting-edge domains offers an exciting frontier where creativity meets technology, and I am eager to explore the endless possibilities they present.

My passion for computer science and mathematics has provided me with a strong foundation to delve into the complexities of AI algorithms and game design principles. The rigorous coursework at UIUC has honed my analytical skills and problem-solving abilities, which I believe are crucial in tackling the challenges posed by AI integration in modern game development.

As I look towards the future, I am inspired by the rapid advancements in AI-driven game mechanics, procedural content generation, and immersive gaming experiences. The prospect of contributing to this dynamic industry, where innovation is constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible, fills me with excitement and motivation to further develop my skills and knowledge in these areas.
