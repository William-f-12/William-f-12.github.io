About William Lu

I am a Senior at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, pursuing a B.S. in Mathematics & Computer Science. My academic path began at UW-Madison and led me to UIUC, where I've developed a deep interest in Machine Learning and Multi-Agent Systems.

My recent work spans building LangGraph-based multi-agent frameworks, developing LLM-driven conversational environments, and optimizing parallel GPU kernels with CUDA. I'm drawn to problems where mathematical rigor meets systems engineering — whether it's designing efficient parallel algorithms or modeling emergent agent behaviors.

Outside of code, I enjoy reading detective novels, photography, and video games — the last of which originally sparked my interest in game development and interactive AI. I'm always looking to collaborate on challenging projects at the intersection of AI, systems, and creative technology.
